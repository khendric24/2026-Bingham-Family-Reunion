Find Someone Who… — Host Instructions

Bingham Family Reunion 2026 · Mixer Game

Before the Game
1
Print one card per person. There are 4 different card variations — mix them up so people at the same table have different cards.
2
Give everyone a pen. You may want to offer a small prize for the winner (or top three finishers).
3
Allow 15–25 minutes for circulation. This works best early in the reunion, before or during a casual meal, as an icebreaker.
During the Game
4
Each clue is a fact about one of the pioneer ancestors. Players find a different family member who knows the answer to each clue — that person signs the square.
5
The same person can only sign each card twice — this forces people to spread out and meet more family members.
6
The FREE square in the middle — sign your own name.
Winning
7
First to complete a row, column, or diagonal calls "Found It!" and wins. Verify 2–3 signatures are legitimate. Award the prize and keep playing for 2nd and 3rd place!
8
After the game, use the answers as conversation starters: "Did you know our ancestor received a sword from Joseph Smith?" These stories deserve to be told.
